Lemon Iced Tea

Tea made from fresh lemons, this iced tea is brisk, perfectly sweet, and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 0.25 cups sugar
  • 5 black tea sachets
  • Juice from 3 lemons

Instructions

  1. Use a citrus juicer to make juicing the lemons easier. Make sure to strain out the pulp and seeds. Set aside.
  2. Bring 4 cups of water to a simmer and turn off heat. Seep tea packets for 5 mins
  3. Stir sugar into the hot tea until completely dissolved
  4. Add lemon juice and shake
  5. Serve over ice with a lemon wedge
