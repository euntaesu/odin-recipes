Spring Rolls

Easy Chinese Spring Rolls Recipe

These crispy snacks are a real crowd-pleaser and always so popular at parties

Ingredients

  • 150-200 g Prawns/Shrimp
  • 15 sheets Spring roll paper
  • 200 g Cabbage
  • 3-4 Dried shiitake mushroom
  • 100 g Carrot
  • 1 tbsp Garlic
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying
  • 2 tsbp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Plain flour ( mixed with 2 tbsp water ) flour glue to seal the wrappers

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop the prawns and thinly shred the carrot and cabbage. Pre-soaked the dry shiitake mushroom in warm water for 15 minutes, then gently squeeze the water out and cut into small pieces.
  2. Heat the large wok/pan over medium high heat, drizzle 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil, add the chopped garlic and stir for few seconds.
  3. Add prawns and stir for few seconds, then follow with the mushroom and shredded carrots. Stir fry for few seconds and add the shredded cabbage then follow with spring onions. ( Stir fry over medium-high heat. )
  4. To wrap the spring rolls, scoop 2-3 tablespoon of fillings on one side of the wrapper. Fold away from your side like an envelope. Seal it up with the plain flour and water mixture glue. Repeat the process to all rolls until the fillings runs out.
  5. Heat the vegetable oil or neutral flavour oil into high heat about 340-350Â°F. Deep fry the spring rolls for 5-6 minutes, or until crispy golden brown. ( Do not overcrowd the oil and keep the temperature into high-heat to prevent the spring rolls from greasy and soggy. )
